## Mindustry: Cosmic Out
[Mindustry: Cosmic Out](https://github.com/LaoHuaJiOfficial/MindustryCosmicOut) is a mod-lib i made recently. Inspired by Factorio: Space Age and Oxygen Not Included's Spaced Out, this mod provided a playable star map, and some advanced launch/landing pads (pads for liquid, payload and planetary logistics variants).
this mod makes it possible to send liquid cargo and payload cargo from sector to another sector, planet to another planet. you can even view planetary logistic real-time on planetary logistics.
currently its still a lib, not aim to provide a full game play. but if anyone who is willing to use this lib as dependency to make mods, plz let me know (for checking the mod's function and provide necessary help)
